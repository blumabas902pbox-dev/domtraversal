An Interactive DOM Traversal

Instructions:

1. Select a Node First! (Grandparent, Parent, Child.) and press once.

2. If Grandparent: Press traverse down to trace its children but cannot traverse up, find a sibling.

3. If Parent: Press traverse up to find the lost parent. Press traverse down to locate the mischievous child. Press next or previous sibling if had one. Especially, adding another child to the family isn't that fun.

4. If Child: Press traverse up to discover its ancestry. Find your sibling by next and prev sibling.

5. Take Note: Grandparent cannot be deleted. A child cannot have a child.
